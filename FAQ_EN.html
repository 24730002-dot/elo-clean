Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I cancel my membership?▼

    After logging in, click the "Cancel Membership" button at the bottom of the "My Classroom > Edit My Info" page. Please note that all learning history will be deleted upon withdrawal.

  • I forgot my ID or password. How can I find it?▼

    On the login page, click the "Find ID/Password" link and follow the identity verification process.

  • Where do I apply for courses?▼

    In the "Online Education" menu, select the desired course, and then click the "Apply for Course" button on the course page.

  • How do I get my certificate?▼

    After completing the course, you can receive it via the "Issue Certificate" button next to the course on the "My Classroom" page.

  • What is the refund policy?▼

    A full refund is available within 7 days of payment, provided you have not started the course. For details, please refer to "Support > Terms of Use".
